House Rules

Guidelines for an unforgettable evening

🤝

Consent is Everything

Ask before initiating any physical contact. "No" and "not right now" are complete sentences.

🔒

What Happens Here, Stays Here

No photos or recordings without explicit permission. Respect everyone's privacy.

🍸

Drink Responsibly

Enjoy yourself, but stay present. Impaired individuals cannot give meaningful consent.

💬

Communicate Openly

Express your boundaries clearly. If something feels off, speak up or find a host.

✨

Embrace the Vibe

Be playful, be curious, be kind. Leave judgment at the door.
